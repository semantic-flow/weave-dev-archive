## Thesis

- 
- Referenced semantic web data wants to be immutable and identifiers must persist, but data often needs to be updated and "identifier sense" can evolve.
  - first-class support: RDF needs a way to differentiate between an artifact in general, different snapshots of an artifact over time, particular byte-arrangements of an artifact (manifestations), and likely retrieval locations.
- Additionally, Semantic Flow attempts to address a twenty-year-old conundrum known as httpRange-14: does a given IRI identify content; or do they identity things, or the "sense" of a thing.
